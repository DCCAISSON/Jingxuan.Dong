I am a PhD candidate in Agricultural Economics and Management at Zhejiang University. I received my M.A. in Agricultural & Forestry Economics and Management from Hainan University in June 2024, and my B.A. with Distinction in Logistics Management from Southeast University in June 2020.

My primary research interests include rural development, the digital economy, and human capital.
