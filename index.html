About Me

Jingxuan Dong is a PhD candidate in Agricultural Economics and Management at Zhejiang University. He received his M.A. in Agricultural & Forestry Economics and Management from Hainan University in June 2024, and his B.A. with Distinction in Logistics Management from Southeast University in June 2020. His primary research interests include rural development, the digital economy, and human capital. Prospective collaborators and students are welcome to get in touch.
